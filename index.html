Our Story

Brunel Sports Centre has grown into one of the most complete university sport offerings in London, built around a simple idea: every student and member of staff should have a place to train, compete, and belong - regardless of experience level. From a fully fitted gym and indoor sports halls to fitness studios and more than fifty student-run clubs, the Centre supports everything from first-time gym-goers to athletes training for national competition.

Facilities

Gym, sports halls, fitness studios, squash courts, and outdoor pitches.

Clubs

50+ student-run sports clubs across performance and recreational levels.

Access

Open to students, staff, and members of the wider community.
